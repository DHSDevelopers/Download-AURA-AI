FAQ

Common Questions

Is AURA really free forever? +
Yes — completely free, no hidden fees, no subscriptions, no credit card ever required. AURA is and will always be free.
Is my data safe? +
Absolutely. All chat history is stored locally on your device. Nothing is shared, sold, or uploaded to any server. Your data belongs to you.
What AI model powers AURA? +
AURA uses Meta's Llama 3.3 70B model hosted on Groq's LPU infrastructure — one of the fastest and most capable free AI models available.
Does it work without internet? +
An internet connection is required for AI features (chat, image analysis) since responses come from Groq's API. The app itself launches offline.
Who built AURA? +
AURA was built by Daniyal Hasan — a self-taught developer and astrophysicist who believes AI should be accessible to everyone, for free.
How do I report a bug? +
Open a GitHub issue on the repository or email directly. Every bug report is taken seriously and fixed as quickly as possible.
